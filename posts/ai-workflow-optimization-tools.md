## What are AI Workflow Optimization Tools?
AI workflow optimization tools are software applications that use AI and machine learning to analyze and optimize business workflows. Some popular AI workflow optimization tools include process mining software, robotic process automation (RPA) tools, and business process management (BPM) systems.
