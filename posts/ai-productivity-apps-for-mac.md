## Introduction to AI Productivity Apps
AI productivity apps are designed to augment human capabilities, providing intelligent assistance and automating tasks that would otherwise consume a significant amount of time. These apps can help Mac users manage their schedules, prioritize tasks, and stay organized. By integrating AI into their workflow, Mac users can experience a significant boost in productivity and efficiency.

For example, a user can set up a Zap to automatically save email attachments to a specific folder on their Mac, eliminating the need for manual intervention.
